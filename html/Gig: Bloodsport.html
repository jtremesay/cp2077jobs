Walkthrough[]

This article requires cleanup.
[Reason: Walkthrough must be written from a third-person perspective and not a gameplay walkthrough]

Roh is being held in the basement of a Tyger Claws dojo. The building surrounded by a small walled courtyard, with one guard posted at the entrance and several more inside. The the courtyard is a public area, so the exterior guard doesn't react to V's presence in the courtyard. On the left side of the building is a side door that requires a Body check to open. On the roof above that door is a window that requires a Technical check to open. Elsewhere on the roof is a window that doesn't require any check to open.

Upon reaching the basement, there will be two cameras along with a couple more gangers, one of which who will be beating Roh, while the other is in a different room watching TV. Neutralise the guard beating Roh and lead him out of the building. The Tyger Claw at the entrance to the courtyard will be gone when you leave the area.

Roh moves quite slowly, so V will have to wait for him. When you reach the Regina's transport, he stops to sit on a bench and asks V who paid to get him out. V can tell him it was Macidew or say that it's a secret. If one tells him who it was, Roh seems surprised and expresses mixed feelings because Macidew is the reason he got picked up to begin with. V advises that it's a good idea if Roh and Macidew don't see each other again, and Roh agrees.

If V rescues Roh without tripping the alarms, Regina is impressed and includes a bonus payment for V to pick up at a drop point.

Journal Entry[]

Matter's urgent. My client, Macidew Coolidge (you might recognize him from his show, "Boxathon," on N54 from a few years back) is looking for someone to get his coach back from the Tyger Claws.
Here's the deal. Macidew got himself into a debt hole. Looking to climb out the easy way, he cut a deal with the Tygers. They gave him a wad of cash to go down in the next fight. And what did he do with it? He goes to the bookie incognito and bets on his own win. Next thing you know, he KO's his opponent in the second round and blasts off into the sunset in an AV.
It was happily ever after for Macidew, until they kidnapped his coach, Roh Chi-Won. They're threatening to kill him if Macidew doesn't come back to Night City. Macidew doesn't want his coach's death to weigh on his conscience, so he got in touch with me and I got in touch with you. Sending you the coords for where they're keeping Roh Chi-Won. Try getting to him without tripping the alarm. Hostages and shootouts don't mix well. Good luck.

Objectives[]

  • Go to the Tyger Claws' dojo.

  • Find out where Roh is being held.
  • Set Roh free.
  • Talk to Roh.
  • Help Roh get outside.
  • Escort Roh to the fixer's transport.
